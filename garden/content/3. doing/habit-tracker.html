habit tracker

a garden grows one day at a time.

why

i don't track habits to optimize myself. i track them to stay honest. it's easy to think you're doing the work when you're not. the calendar doesn't lie. consistency is what makes writing online and tending a garden work.

these six things keep me grounded: stillness, rest, movement, learning, reflection, and play. when i do them consistently, everything else falls into place. when i don't, it's easy to notice.
