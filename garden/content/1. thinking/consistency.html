consistency

the unsexy secret behind everything that works.

showing up matters more than showing off. a garden grows one day at a time. so does a body, a skill, a body of work.

the habit-tracker exists because consistency is hard to feel but easy to measure. the calendar doesn't lie.

why it's hard

  • results lag behind effort (you don't see progress immediately)
  • motivation fades faster than discipline builds
  • life interrupts — travel, illness, chaos

why it matters

writing online only works if you keep doing it. tending a garden only works if you tend it. chess only improves if you play and review. the compound effect is real, but it requires the reps.

"we are what we repeatedly do. excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit." — aristotle (via will durant)
