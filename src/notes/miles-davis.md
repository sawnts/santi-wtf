kept reinventing what jazz could be. cool jazz → hard bop → modal → fusion.

ultimate [integration](/notes/integration/) — pulling in rock, electronics, funk.

> "do not fear mistakes. there are none."
> — miles davis
<hr>
a fan once complained to miles davis after a show: "that new shit you're into, i just can't get with it."

miles clapped back: "should i wait for you?"

you don't need everyone to get it. you need to keep moving.

<hr>
miles davis told his musicians: "if you learn any of that old shit, you're fired."

he was talking about his own celebrated work.

he knew he couldn't step into the future without letting go of his past.

don't let your past keep you from stepping into your future.
