a running list of things i'm learning this year. updated as life teaches me.

<hr>
**going to bed early is just sleeping in on the front end.** it's a proactive win, not a loss of time.

**process is personal.** don't confuse someone else's map for the territory. look for inspiration, not instructions. the only way out is through the work. [the map is not the territory](/notes/the-map-is-not-the-territory/).

**on personal knowledge management:** no loose quotes. no digital hoarding. if i keep it, i must define *why* it matters and *where* it fits. 

**if you're not writing, you're not thinking.** writing forces you to face yourself because it exposes the flaws of your thinking. (see: [the power of writing online](/notes/power-of-writing-online/)).

**you want your goals to be perfect and big and doable but you can only pick two.** 

**laziness makes the most exciting and beautiful boring and dull.**

**creativity is a battle** where the ego wants to keep you "safe" in captivity, while the soul wants you to be free.

**tool-switching is a drug.** it creates the illusion of progress without the output.

**you can't experience safety without first practicing courage.**

**the healing we do independently is preparation for the healing we do in relationship.** it's easy to be "healed" in isolation. the real test of emotional maturity comes when you finally let yourself be seen.

**routine isn't the only path to peace.** your peace comes from *being*, not optimizing.

**movement is medicine for the body. meditation is medicine for the mind. writing is medicine for the soul.**

the healing power of creativity isn't in the end product, it's in the process
