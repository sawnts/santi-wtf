evergreen notes aren't content. they're tools for thought.

you don't write them to publish. you write them to understand. [to connect ideas](/notes/linking-is-thinking/). to build a web of knowledge you can traverse, wrestle with, and expand.

the goal isn't a polished output — it's clarity. do your best to capture the idea in its purest form, link it to related concepts, and [let the connections do the work.](/notes/making-effective-connections/)
