Integration Thinking

üå≥ evergreen ¬∑ planted: nov 12, 2024 ¬∑ tended: jan 18, 2026
creativity thinking-modes

integration is the act of combining two or more things that don't obviously belong together. it's the first of my four creative thinking modes.

the magic happens when the combination creates something that couldn't have been predicted from either part alone. miles davis integrated jazz with rock electronics. david ogilvy integrated research with creative copywriting.

"creativity is just connecting things." ‚Äî steve jobs

integration differs from splitting in that you're building up rather than breaking down. both are generative, but they move in opposite directions.

the key question: what two things, combined, would create more than their sum?
