essays are for readers. evergreen notes are for thinking.

an essay is a polished, complete piece meant for external publication — blog posts, newsletters, articles. it has a beginning, middle, and end. status moves from draft to published.

an evergreen note is an atomic idea meant for internal use. it's a building block you link to and combine with other notes. status reflects maturity: seed → sprout → evergreen.

essays tell stories. evergreen notes capture truths.
