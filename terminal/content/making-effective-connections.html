making effective connections

good links are like good friendships. they need shared context to mean something.

linking two notes because they both mention "creativity" is shallow. no different than introducing two people just because they live in new york.

effective connections happen when ideas speak to each other. when one note challenges, extends, or illuminates another. when the link itself carries meaning.

the best connections surprise you. they reveal relationships you didn't see when you wrote the notes. that's when the system starts thinking with you.
