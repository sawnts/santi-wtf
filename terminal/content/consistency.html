thoughts on consistency

consistency is the unsexy secret behind everything that works.

showing up matters more than showing off. a garden grows one day at a time. so does a body, a skill, a body of work.

why is being consistent so damn hard?

  • results lag behind effort (you don't see progress immediately)
  • motivation fades faster than discipline builds
  • life happens — travel, illness, chaos

why it matters

writing online only works if you keep doing it. tending a garden only works if you tend it. taste only improves if you develop it. the compound effect is real, but it requires consistent reps.

"we are what we repeatedly do. excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit." — aristotle
